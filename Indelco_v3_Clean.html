Total $ paid this period, net of credits/returns

On-time receipts vs original committed date

Mean variance from committed to actual receipt
LATE LINES
Lines arriving >2 days past commitment
AVG LEAD TIME
Calendar days from PO placement to receipt
PRICE VARIANCE
Unit price change vs prior period (weighted)
